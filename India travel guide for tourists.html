India

India is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. It is known for its diversity, offering a variety of landscapes and cultures. Some cities, like Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, and Visakhapatnam, are located along the sea coast. Others, such as Indore, Bengaluru, and Pune, are situated at higher altitudes.

Hill stations like Shillong, Mussoorie, and Kullu provide serene environments, while Rajasthan offers stunning desert landscapes. India’s beauty is enhanced by its rich cultures, such as Tamil Nadu's Kathakali dancers and Maharashtra's traditional Marathi dance.

Popular attractions include the Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park in Pune, Marina Beach in Chennai, and Mysore Palace in Mysuru. While India is generally warm, it is cooler than Dubai, making it a comfortable destination for many tourists.
