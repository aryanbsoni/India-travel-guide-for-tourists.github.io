The fort was stolen of its artwork and jewels during Nader Shah's invasion of the Mughal Empire in 1739. Most of the fort's marble structures were later on destroyed by the British following the Indian Rebellion of 1857. The fort's defensive walls were largely undamaged and the fortress was later on used as a military force. The prime ministers always raise the Indian Flag ever since the 15th August of 1947.
